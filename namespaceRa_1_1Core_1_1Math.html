Returns the sum of two quaternions, resolving antipodality by flipping the sign of q2 if q1.q2 is negative.

This operation is usually denoted as a circled + sign, forming the basis of the QLERP algorithm. See "Spherical Blend Skinning" (Kavan & Zara 2005) for more details.

Decompose a given rotation Qin into a swing rotation and a twist rotation.

Qswing is a rotation whose axis lies in the XY plane and Qtwist is a rotation about axis Z. such as Qin = Qswing * Qtwist If the rotation is already around axis z, Qswing will be set to identity and Qtwist equal to Qin
